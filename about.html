freida's story


Freida Margolis sits on a quaint corner of West Lynn. What was once a butcher for 117 years - it's now a place of whimsy and conviviality - with the vibes of live music and vinyl on the stereo - it's just what it is - friendly and genuine.

We're a great little neighbourhood bar with a backstreets of Bogota ambience. Loyal locals sit outside with their well-behaved dogs, supping on sangria, wine and craft beer, and enjoying eclectic sounds from the owner's big vinyl collection.

Most weekends we have a three piece band or a soulful singer contributing rhythmic notes from the corner – most of which hail from weird and wonderful places around the globe. It’s always surprising and more importantly, entertaining to watch a live performance here.
